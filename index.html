Most marketing agencies run your ads and disappear. You get leads. Nobody follows up. The calendar stays half empty.

The System

Two parts. One complete machine.

1 Acquisition

Ads that build trust — not discount hunters.

Most med spa ads look like this — $149 Botox, 50% off filler, limited time offer. Those ads attract price shoppers who leave the moment someone offers $10 less. We run education-based ads instead. Your injector goes on camera and speaks directly to what patients are actually afraid of. Looking frozen. Looking fake. Not knowing what to expect. That kind of ad builds trust before anyone clicks. Patients choose you for your expertise — not your price.

30–50 qualified new patient leads every month
2 Conversion

The moment a lead comes in — the AI takes over.

Your front desk is busy. Checking patients in, answering phones, processing payments. Following up on a new lead within 5 minutes while doing all of that — simply not possible. So leads go cold. And you never even know it happened. Our AI fixes this completely. The moment someone fills in your lead form — the AI calls them. In under 60 seconds. Not a text. Not an email. An actual phone call. Day or night. Whether your clinic is open or completely closed.

No lead ever sits unanswered again
